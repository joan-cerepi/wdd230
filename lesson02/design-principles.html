Visual Hierarchy

Netflix

netflix.com

Netflix skillfully employs varying font sizes to create a visually appealing and easily navigable interface. The strategic use of larger fonts for headings draws immediate attention, providing a clear hierarchy of information. Smaller fonts that support details maintain a harmonious balance, and prevent visual clutter. The deliberate play with font sizes enhances the overall readability and ensures that users can quickly identify and focus on key elements, contributing to a seamless and enjoyable browsing experience.

White Space and Clean Design

Apple

apple.com

Apple's website keeps things looking fresh with its smart use of space and clean design. The developers did not overdo it. They've got this apparent knack for spacing things out, so it doesn't feel cramped. Navigating around is easy thanks to the clean, organized setup. The amount of content is just right and does not overwhelm the user, ensuring a better experience.

PARC: Contrast

BristolBeacon

bristolbeacon.org

Bristol Beacon's website impressively plays with contrast, by adding a dynamic and eye-catching touch to its design. The clever use of contrasting colors and shades creates a vibrant visual experience. Their color choices make important elements pop, guiding your eyes to key information effortlessly. The intentional use of contrast enhances readability and makes it easy for visitors to focus on what matters.
